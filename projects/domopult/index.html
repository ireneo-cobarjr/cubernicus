Domopult

Designing an app for different residential services.

The challenge

As the tech world marches on, we’re constantly streamlining our lives to spend less time on those little details which can be taken care of between more important activities. That’s exactly the type of app we made for automating the management of an apartment complex or cottage settlement. Our objective was to design a product that didn’t just ensure the management’s job, but which also resolved all the concerns and questions of residents in a totally modern way.

The project

Domopult provides a work space for multiple user categories. The management company wants to ensure timely payments for housing and community services. Residents want to resolve any issues that might arise in their complex in a way that’s convenient for them, with reciprocal communication instead of the hassle of one-way complaints to management. Domopult smartly takes all these functions into account.
