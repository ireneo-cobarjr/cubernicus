Appcity

Designing the first “Smart City” app for Latin America.

The challenge

A client from Latin America got in touch about developing an application that would serve as a digital city guide to Santiago. We created a light touch design, emphasizing functional elements and interactivity. The app stands out with its own unique aesthetic, and the animations improve user interaction.

The project

AppCity is an informational guide which spotlights Santiago’s most exciting attractions. The app is an obvious game-changer for the tourism industry, but it’s so much more than that. It keeps tabs on various events and makes it super easy to track activities you like and add them to your selections.
